Update Templates - How To Use

Step One

After you've installed the app and open the Update Templates item view, select which category you would like your first template posted to. The info popups on each button describe the difference. "Board" will post a template that is visible to all members of this board. "User" will post a template that is only to you, but available to post across all boards when you click on the "User" section.

Step Two

After you've chosen a category for you template, use our rich text editor to create the content of your template.

Step Three

After you've created the content of your template, click to "Post" button to post the template to the category of your choice.

Step Four

After posting, your template is now available to copy to your clipboard, or delete with one click.
